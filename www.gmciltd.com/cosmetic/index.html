INDUSTRY

COSMETIC & PERSONAL CARE

OUR COMPETENCE

For some of the leading companies in the cosmetics industry, GMCI manufactures a wide variety of raw materials for cosmetics and personal care.

GMCI has a broad variety of products for the personal care sector, including functioning products like cleansers and conditioners, aesthetic modificatory and active materials, with a selection of personal care mineral and acute cosmetic. Over 60 categories of applications include skin, hair, colour cosmetics, sun care, oral care and fragrance products and many more in all segments of the market.

Cosmetics is an irrevocable part of everybody’s everyday lives. The need of cosmetics to invent and introduce new products on a regular base is driven by increased demands for high performance and low toxicity.

GMCI offers a range of raw material minerals to choose from, i.e. Talc, Carbonate, Calcium Carbonate, Kaolin, Mica, Bentonite, Cosmetic Clay, Quartz, Titanium Dioxide is produced according to international regulatory standards under clean and controlled conditions of manufacture.

GMCI specializes in the procurement of top cosmetic & personal care ingredients, so you can easily email our mineral expert at info@gmciltd.com if you do not see what product you need.
